About modmixx

A music sharing space for creators.

What is modmixx?

modmixx is a prototype music sharing platform built for people who create, tinker, and experiment. It's a home for producers, songwriters, beatmakers, and anyone who loves exploring sound.

Here, you can upload your tracks, share works-in-progress, and connect with others who understand the joy (and sometimes frustration) of making music. It's about process as much as finished products: a space to grow, experiment, and learn alongside peers.

Fun fact: The name "modmixx" comes from modular synthesizers - those beautiful walls of dials, cables, and endless possibilities. Just like patching together modules to create unique sounds, our platform connects creators to build and mixx something greater together.

This is just the beginning. As modmixx evolves, we'll introduce:

  • Free production tips and resources
  • Monthly producer challenges to push your creativity
  • Masterclasses and insights from working pros

Whether you're a beginner learning the ropes or a seasoned producer looking for community, modmixx is here to give you a stage before the spotlight.

Community Guidelines

How we mixx together

At modmixx, we believe music communities thrive when they're supportive, inclusive, and honest. To keep this space safe and inspiring for everyone:

  • Respect all voices. Treat every member with kindness. We value diversity of background, genre, and skill level.
  • Share with care. If feedback is invited, be constructive: highlight strengths, suggest paths forward, and always frame it as your perspective.
  • Encourage freely. If feedback isn't requested, celebrate what you enjoy. A little encouragement can go a long way.
  • Support growth. Remember that analytical listening and thoughtful critique help everyone; both the creator and the listener to grow.
  • Keep it positive. The goal is to leave others energised to create more, not second-guess their voice.
By following these guidelines, you're helping us build a culture where creativity comes first, and where every artist has the space to explore and be heard.
